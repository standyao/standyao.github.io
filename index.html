Li Yao

I am a post doc advised by Prof. Byungkook Lim in biological sciences at University of California San Diego (UCSD), Before joining UCSD from 2022, I recieved my Ph.D degree in cognitive neuroscience from Beijing Normal University (BNU)

I'm interested in local and long-range circuit mechanisms underlying mental disorders and neurodegenerative diseases (especially cortical-stritum-thalamic loop for movement control and Parkinson's disease).

My resesarch involves recording synaptic transmission and large-scale neural dynamic changes during behaviors. It included both in vitro (loose-patch and whole-cell) and in vivo (two photon calcium imaging and silicon probes recording) electrophysiology.
